How Scores Are Calculated
🎯 Theme Match Score
Base formula: theme_projects × 30
Counts how many projects the researcher has done in the predicted theme.
Example: 9 theme projects = 9 × 30 = 270 points
🔍 Keyword Match Score
Uses TF-IDF similarity between your project title and researcher's past titles.
• Exact/near-exact match (≥85% similar): similarity × 200
• Partial match (>20% similar): similarity × 50
• Low similarity (≤20%): 0 points
Example: Perfect title match = 1.0 × 200 = 200 points
👥 Contribution Score
Base formula: min(total_projects × 5, 50)
Rewards researchers with more overall experience working with at least one another researchers (capped at 50 points).
Example: 10+ total projects = 50 points (max)
⏰ Recency Score
Base formula: recent_theme_projects × 10
Counts theme-relevant projects from the last 3 years (2023-2026).
Example: 3 recent projects in same theme = 3 × 10 = 30 points
Final Score: (Theme × weight) + (Keyword × weight) + (Contribution × weight) + (Recency × weight)
